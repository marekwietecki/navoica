Nederland sauce

Emulsion, whipped sauce based on butter and egg yolks.

Nederland sauce prescription

  1. Melt the butter
  2. In the water baths whip egg yolks until fluffy
  3. Gradually add butter mixing it all the time
  4. When sauce will be smooth consistency, take bowl off the pot
  5. Season with salt and pepper
